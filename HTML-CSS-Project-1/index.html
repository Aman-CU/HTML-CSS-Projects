DR. A.P.J Abdul kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, popularly known as Missile Man of India, was a visionary scientist, educator and statesman who left an indelible mark on India’s space and missile programs. In the realm of India’s scientific and technological legacy, a singular figure shines as a symbol of inspiration, creativity and unwavering commitment to the nation’s advancement: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, fondly known as the “Missile Man of India.” His life and significant contributions serve as a radiant paradigm for future generations of Indians and the global scientific community, guiding the way toward success.
